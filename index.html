VISUAL ART

Alex Cunningham is a self-taught visual artist originally from Saint John, New Brunswick, and currently residing in Fredericton. While she attends school in philosophy, she has been painting and developing her skills over the past seven years.

Her work consistently takes the form of portraits, whether or people or of animals.
